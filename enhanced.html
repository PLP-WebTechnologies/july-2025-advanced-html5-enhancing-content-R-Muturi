Graphic Design

Graphic design is a profession, academic discipline and applied art that involves creating visual communications intended to transmit specific messages to social groups, with specific objectives in mind. Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content.

Graphic Design Elements

Hierachy

  • Guides the viewer's eye
  • Organizes Information
  • Effective Communication
  • Uses Visual Cues

Typography

  1. Typeface and font.
  2. Readability and legibility.
  3. Spacing.
  4. Font pairing.
  5. Use of white space
